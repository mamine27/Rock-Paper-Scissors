About

In this version of Rock Paper Scissors, you get to challenge the computer! Just like the classic game, you can choose between rock, paper, or scissors, and the computer will randomly make its move. The rules remain the same: rock crushes scissors, scissors cut paper, and paper covers rock. It's a fun way to test your luck and see if you can outsmart the computer in this timeless game of chance and strategy. Whether you’re just looking for a quick game or want to hone your skills, our Rock Paper Scissors game offers endless excitement!
